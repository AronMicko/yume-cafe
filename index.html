Our Story

Yume means dream.

We started Yume Cafe with one focus: ceremonial-grade matcha and houjicha, prepared the way it's meant to be, whisked by hand, no syrupy shortcuts, just the plant and a little patience.

Every cup is made to order in our little corner of Quiapo. Come for the matcha, stay for the quiet.

Try whisking the bowl, real matcha takes about 15 seconds of wrist work to bloom a proper froth.
